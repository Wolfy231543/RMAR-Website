Welcome to RMAR!

What are we?

RMAR is a small "organization" where we can show our personal projects to the world. RMAR used to be exclusively in the game Rec Room before it's servers shut down. Now, we are more than just one person and have nothing to do with Rec Room. Most of our stuff is on Discord, this website is just to show off some things. If you want to keep updated on RMAR projects, talk with people about RMAR or it's projects, or many other things such as report issues, you can find all of that and more in the RMAR discord.

You can join the Discord server with the button at the top of the page!
